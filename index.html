About Me

I have more than enough experiences in mold design and machining for a lot of either known companies and small scale companies. Many of them are international footwear brands, including Nike™, Adidas™, OnRunning™, Asics™, Northface™, K2™, Discovery™, National Geographics™, and more.

I also have experiences in Parts Production Planning and Machining for automotive industry (truck spare parts) that covers Japan and Korea automotive brands like Hino™, Nissan™, Mitsubishi™, and Hyundai™.

In addition, I've worked on many projects for small scale manufacturing companies that produce plastics molds for motorcycle parts and household appliances using injection plastic molds or die-cast molds.
